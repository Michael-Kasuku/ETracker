Privacy Policy

At ETracker, we are committed to safeguarding your personal information and ensuring transparency. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, protect, and share your data in compliance with relevant data protection laws, including Kenya's Data Protection Act, 2019, and the Constitution of Kenya, 2010.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information to enhance our tracking services, including:

  • Personal Identification: Name, email, phone number, and account details.
  • Device Information: IMEI numbers, serial numbers, and specifications.
  • Location Data: Real-time and historical tracking via GPS/GSM.
  • Usage Data: Access times, IP addresses, device details, and browser type.
  • Payment Information: Secure payment processing details.

2. Use of Your Information

We utilize your information for:

  • Providing real-time device tracking.
  • Notifying users of security threats.
  • Securing transactions and processing payments.
  • Analyzing trends for better performance.
  • Meeting legal and regulatory requirements.

3. Data Security

We employ robust security measures such as encryption and secure servers to protect your data. However, no online platform can guarantee absolute security.

4. Your Rights

You have rights regarding your data, including:

  • Accessing and obtaining a copy of your data.
  • Requesting corrections for inaccuracies.
  • Requesting data deletion where applicable.
  • Restricting or objecting to data processing.
  • Requesting data portability for transfers.

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@etracker.com.

5. Updates to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated via email or platform notifications.
